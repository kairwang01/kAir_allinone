Local-first — your command surface stays on your iPhone.

On-device models · Controlled memory · No account in v1

Routing, memory, and capability execution are designed to run on your device. In v1, prompts and on-device data are not sent to kAir servers.

On-device processing

Inference runs on Apple Foundation Models, right on your iPhone.

Nothing leaves your device

Your chats and Apple Health data are processed locally and are not sent to kAir servers in this version.

No account, no tracking

No sign-up required. No ads, no cross-app tracking, and no analytics profile built about you.

You are always in control

Any export is something you start, preview, and confirm. It is never automatic and never silent.

What kAir does not do

  • Send your chats to a server
  • Export your Apple Health data
  • Show you ads
  • Track you across apps
  • Require an account to work
  • Upload your data to train models

v1 ships Chat + Health. More capabilities unlock over time.
